Help & Support

How do I report a problem?

Go to the 'Report an Issue' section, fill in the required details, and submit. Your report will be reviewed by the EcoTrack team.

How do I give feedback?

Visit the 'Feedback' section, provide your suggestions or comments, and submit. Your feedback helps improve the portal.

How do I use the website?

Use the navigation menu to report issues, give feedback, and check the status of your submissions. The interface is simple and self-explanatory.

How long does it take for my report or feedback to be considered?

The EcoTrack team reviews submissions regularly. Typical feedback/report processing may take a few days depending on the volume.
